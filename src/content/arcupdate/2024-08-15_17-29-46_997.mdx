If you’ve been with us for a while, you’ll know that we love fine-tuning and tweaking Arc for you at Browser HQ. _Sometimes it’s big, other times it’s tiny._ 

Recently, though, there haven’t been _that many_ big new features landing in your Arc. 

And yet the team is humming, prototyping, dreaming.

In fact, [**right now**](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=ad7d146247&e=2d91f117eb) the gap between how much we’ve been shipping, and how large we’ve been dreaming has never felt _wider_. So, while the team is going hard in the code and pixels, **we want to share this part of the journey with you, too**.

Read on to learn more about our new weekly voice note experiment **[_Imagining Arc_](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=ef1f7f5275&e=2d91f117eb)** - and this week’s updates!

**🌐 Native Ad Block is coming soon**  

We believe a cleaner internet is a better internet. One without the popups, ads, banners that get in the way of focusing on the things that matter most. For those of us with tech-challenged friends or family, a single popup ad could mean countless hours of free tech support, _courtesy of you_.  
  
Plus, with [Chrome’s transition to Manifest V3](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=64535b651a&e=2d91f117eb), _[many](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=c37d4ae49c&e=2d91f117eb) [of](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=33f66c198e&e=2d91f117eb) [you](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=64917d80f6&e=2d91f117eb)_ were asking how we plan to keep Arc clear of the clutter.  
  
Well, great news! The dream team - Mateusz, Omar, Brian, Andrzej, and Miko - are working hard on bringing native Ad Block to you! It’s still baking, but once it's out and the icing’s on, we’ll have it straight over to you.
