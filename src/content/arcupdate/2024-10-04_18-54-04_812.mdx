You know what they say, “_if you want to go fast, go alone - if you want to go far, go together_.” And while sometimes it may feel like fast and far can’t coexist, we’re adamant it’s worth trying to do both, with you, at Browser HQ.  
  
Today’s update is all about expanding the Arc world - from opening up the [public beta of **Arc Search for Android**](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=3c8f9a8dbe&e=2d91f117eb), to welcoming **our first class of student ambassadors**.   
  
So read on for a few new updates – as we go fast and far in dreaming up a better way to use the internet, together.

**🧪** **Ready, set, test: join the Arc Search Android Beta!** 

You heard that right – [Arc Search for Android](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=f4983882d4&e=2d91f117eb) is _almost_ ready for prime time. Starting today, anyone who wants to help bring the magic of Arc Search to Android can test the beta:

1.  Head to our [**Google Play page**](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=10de50bd1f&e=2d91f117eb)
2.  Download the **Arc Search app**
3.  Hit the big ✔️ to **opt into the beta**  

Our only request – **let us know what we could do better**! We want to build the best app we can for the Android community and your feedback will help us get there.  
  
Send us reports by heading to the **Arc Search Settings > Contact the Team**.
