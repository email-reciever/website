As the seasons change and leaves turn, it’s only fitting that we keep with the arborist theme and share that _Arc now supports macOS Sequoia_.  
  
Alongside all of you who shared bugs and feedback during the beta, [Patrick](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=4cf8a0b7ad&e=2d91f117eb) has been keeping an eye on the new macOS release, making sure Arc was fully ready. Now - thanks to you and a few fixes from our crew - it finally is.  
  
In Patrick’s own words “we owe you one!”
