That's why we’re beyond excited to finally bring Arc Search to the largest OS on the planet: Android.  
  
After an incredible Open Beta where over 100,000+ Android users took it for a spin, Arc Search is officially open to everyone in the [Google Play Store](https://company.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=73c2f0478f1b3cd8343ae8f45&id=28ac719f09&e=2d91f117eb).

🌐 **Arc Search on Android is officially open to all**

Arc Search has always been about getting you the answers you need without any of the clutter.

 

Starting today, all Android users can now enjoy:

*   **🔎** **Browse for Me** sifts through web pages to deliver quick, clear answers in seconds (now in your native language too).
    
*   **⛔** **Ad Blocking** kicks out unwanted ads, trackers, and popups right from install, so you can tune out the noise.
    
*   **🧹Decluttered tabs.** Inactive tabs auto-archive to keep your space clean of virtual dust bunnies.
    

Plus, thanks to all the amazing feedback we received during beta, we fixed what bugged you most:  
  
You’ll find **full support for Android 12**, **Voice Search**, **Landscape Mode**, and a **cleaner Incognito mode**, plus Android-specific designs like **Adaptable Widgets** and **Custom Icons**.

🌐 **Android 上的 Arc Search 正式向所有人开放**

Arc Search 始终致力于为您提供所需的答案，而不会造成任何混乱。

 

从今天开始，所有 Android 用户现在都可以享受：

* **🔎** **Browse for Me** 筛选网页，在几秒钟内提供快速、清晰的答案（现在也可以使用您的母语）。

* **⛔** **广告拦截** 可在安装后立即踢出不需要的广告、跟踪器和弹出窗口，因此您可以消除噪音。

* **🧹整理标签页。** 非活动标签页自动存档，让您的空间远离虚拟灰尘。

另外，由于我们在测试期间收到的所有令人惊叹的反馈，我们修复了最让您烦恼的问题：

您会发现**完全支持 Android 12**、**语音搜索**、**横向模式**和**更干净的隐身模式**，以及 Android 专用设计，例如**自适应小部件**和**自定义图标**。
