7.  [NexFaster](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/92udw7d2rkanh6lv47vhzuz5l2333fw/l2heh6ulv0kx32h6/aHR0cHM6Ly9uZXhmYXN0ZXIucmRzeC5kZXYv) is a minimal template that integrates React Router within Next.js to produce world peace intuitive, Type-safe client-side routing. It’s 2025 – anything goes.
    
8.  Matt Palmer wrote about how [it’s very easy to hack vibe-coded apps](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/92udw7d2rkanh6lv47vhzuz5l2333fw/m2h7h6u30gn48zum/aHR0cHM6Ly9tYXR0cGFsbWVyLmlvL3Bvc3RzL0NWRS0yMDI1LTQ4NzU3Lw==).
