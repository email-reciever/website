A few cybersecurity academics published a paper called [Cascading Spy Sheets](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/lmum72m06kbmhn0z90vsgu8lo4v00bg/6qhehou74zr38qso/aHR0cHM6Ly9jaXNwYS5kZS9lbi9yZXNlYXJjaC9wdWJsaWNhdGlvbnMvODQxNjItY2FzY2FkaW5nLXNweS1zaGVldHMtZXhwbG9pdGluZy10aGUtY29tcGxleGl0eS1vZi1tb2Rlcm4tY3NzLWZvci1lbWFpbC1hbmQtYnJvd3Nlci1maW5nZXJwcmludGluZw==) about how bad guys can “exploit the complexity of modern CSS for email and browser fingerprinting.” I knew I had a good reason for refusing to learn modern CSS.
