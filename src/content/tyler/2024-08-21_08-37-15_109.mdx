## Cool Bits

1.  [Million.js 3.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/58hvh8umm962vwu6/aHR0cHM6Ly9taWxsaW9uLmRldi9ibG9nL21pbGxpb24tMw==) just launched with faster builds and a faster runtime, so it can “make React faster” even… faster?
    
2.  [Unlayer Embed](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/25h2h9u22omwv5b3/aHR0cHM6Ly91bmxheWVyLmNvbS9lbWJlZC8_c3JjPWJ5dGVz) is a drag-and-drop email editor, page builder, popup builder, and document builder for your SaaS application. It’s white-label, easy to embed, and will easily save you _weeks_ of dev time. \[sponsored\]
    
3.  Hunor Márton Borbély created this [SVG Tutorial Advent Calendar](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/qvh8h8urr6kp4gfl/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdmctdHV0b3JpYWwuY29tLw==) that will help you give yourself the gift of vector knowledge this holiday season.
    
4.  [Electron 28.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/g3hnhwu33zqwldtr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZWxlY3Ryb25qcy5vcmcvYmxvZy9lbGVjdHJvbi0yOC0w) adds support for ES Modules.
    
5.  Nolan Lawson wrote an article called [Let’s learn how modern JavaScript frameworks work by building one](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/9qhzhdupp25r66b9/aHR0cHM6Ly9ub2xhbmxhd3Nvbi5jb20vMjAyMy8xMi8wMi9sZXRzLWxlYXJuLWhvdy1tb2Rlcm4tamF2YXNjcmlwdC1mcmFtZXdvcmtzLXdvcmstYnktYnVpbGRpbmctb25lLw==). Looking forward to part 2 where he walks you through how to use that framework to raise a $2m seed round, defraud your investors, fake your own death, and move to the Caribbean.
    
6.  The Stellate team just announced [Fuse.js](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/3ohphdu77dpg2rhr/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdGVsbGF0ZS5jby9ibG9nL2Fubm91bmNpbmctZnVzZS1qcw==) - an opinionated framework for creating typesafe data layers that immediately gave me flashbacks of watching emo music videos on Fuse TV at my friend’s house in 10th grade.
    
7.  [Codecov](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/n2hohqu33o70x5a6/aHR0cHM6Ly9hYm91dC5jb2RlY292LmlvL2NvZGVjb3YtZnJlZS10cmlhbC8_dXRtX3NvdXJjZT1ieXRlcyZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPXBhaWQtY29tbXVuaXR5JnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj1jb2RlY292LWZ5MjRxNC1ldmVyZ3JlZW4mdXRtX2NvbnRlbnQ9bmV3c2xldHRlci1jb2RlY292b3ZlcmFsbC1mcmVldHJpYWw=) is a coding coverage solution that you’ll actually use. It gives you actionable insights about your PR’s test coverage directly inside a PR comment - so you can always do a quick status check without having to leave your workflow. \[sponsored\]
    
8.  [Reactotron just released v3.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/48hvh7urr2x8pnbx/aHR0cHM6Ly9zaGlmdC5pbmZpbml0ZS5yZWQvYW5ub3VuY2luZy1yZWFjdG90cm9uLTMtMC05MzRiNWEyMjFiZGE=) of their desktop app for inspecting and debugging React Native applications. It’s also the name of the cyborg that always tries to kidnap me in my recurring stress dreams. I’m doing fine.
    
9.  OlegWock wrote [A Guide to React Suspense](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/wnh2h6uwwo06kot7/aHR0cHM6Ly9zaW5qYS5pby9ibG9nL2d1aWRlLXRvLXJlYWN0LXN1c3BlbnNl). It’s an insightful post, but the most disturbing part was when they mentioned that Suspense was first introduced _five (???)_ years ago. I guess Shakespeare was right when he famously wrote that, _The years start comin’ and they don’t stop comin’._
    
10.  The creator of Panda CSS wrote a nuanced article called [How does xXx compare to Panda CSS](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvlz60wa8urke6444/reh8h9u0056z3qu2/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYXN0YWhtZXIuZGV2L3Bvc3RzL2hvdy1kb2VzLXh4eC1jb21wYXJlcy10by1wYW5kYS1jc3M=). Admittedly, I was unaware that xXx and Panda CSS were in the same cinematic universe, but I am happy to see one of Vin Diesel’s best works finally getting the respect it deserves.
