## Pop Quiz: Answer

#### Sponsored by [Infinite Red](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/wvu9xd93o4sghk93rmvslun38mkxxa8/58hvh8um49ddnxs6/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbmZpbml0ZS5yZWQvYnl0ZXM=)

What gets logged to the console?

```js
const res = ["👨", "‍", "👩", "‍", "👧", "‍", "👦"].join('')

console.log(res) // 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
```

It’s the ”👨‍👩‍👧‍👦” emoji.

This works because the Unicode standard supports mapping individual emojis with zero-width joiners to create new emojis. In our case, taking individual emojis and joining them to get a family emoji. Wow, programming.

## 小测验：答案

#### 由\[无限红]赞助(https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/wvu9xd93o4sghk93rmvslun38mkxxa8/58hvh8um49ddnxs6/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbmZpbml0ZS5yZWQvYnl0ZXM=)

控制台记录了什么？

```js
const res = ["👨", "‍", "👩", "‍", "👧", "‍", "👦"].join('')

console.log(res) // 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
```

这是“👨‍👩‍👧‍👦”表情符号。

这是可行的，因为 Unicode 标准支持使用零宽度连接符映射单个表情符号来创建新的表情符号。 在我们的例子中，将单独的表情符号组合起来以获得家庭表情符号。哇，编程。
