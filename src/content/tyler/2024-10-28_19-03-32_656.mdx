## Cool Bits

1.  Jonathan Creamer wrote about how his team at Microsoft [shrunk their JavaScript monorepo git size by 94%](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/6qhehou7m2259wso/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuam9uYXRoYW5jcmVhbWVyLmNvbS9ob3ctd2Utc2hydW5rLW91ci1naXQtcmVwby1zaXplLWJ5LTk0LXBlcmNlbnQv), and now I understand why big tech have to pay developers so much.
    
2.  [Convex Components](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/kkhmh2ulk995x2al/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuY29udmV4LmRldi9jb21wb25lbnRz) are a new set of modular, TypeScript building blocks for your backend that help you confidently set up durable functions, real-time data management, third-party integrations, and more. \[sponsored\]
    
3.  [tinybench just released v3](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/58hvh8umr99n6zt6/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL3RpbnlsaWJzL3RpbnliZW5jaC9yZWxlYXNlcy90YWcvdjMuMC4w) of their simple, tiny, lightweight benchmarking library.
    
4.  Tanner Linsley gave us all [an early glimpse of TanStack Start](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/25h2h9u2koo8mot3/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1BdUhxd1FzZjY0bw==) with this conference talk at Netlify Compose. People were a lot more enthusiastic for this than my neighbors were about the “early glimpse” I gave them of my halloween costume – a moldy Lunchly.
    
5.  [Medusa just released v2.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/qvh8h8ur9664k8ul/aHR0cHM6Ly9tZWR1c2Fqcy5jb20vYmxvZy92Mi1yZWxlYXNlLw==) of its Node.js-powered ecomm platform. It’s a “complete rewrite of Medusa’s architecture and feature set”, so if you love ecommerce _and_ breaking changes, you’re in luck.
    
6.  Udoh wrote this [6-part deep dive on building a Remix app from scratch](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/9qhzhdupo2265da9/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudWRvaGplcmVtaWFoLmNvbS9ibG9nL3JlbWl4LXRvZG8tYXBwLXBhcnQtMS1idWlsZGluZy10aGUtYXBwLWxheW91dC1hbmQtc3RydWN0dXJl), which I hear was just green-lit to be made into a mini-series for the Roku channel.
    
7.  Ian and Manoel wrote about [why it took PostHog so long to build mobile session replay](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/3ohphdu7edd2p4hr/aHR0cHM6Ly9wb3N0aG9nLmNvbS9ibG9nL21vYmlsZS1zZXNzaW9uLXJlcGxheT91dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249Ynl0ZXMmdXRtX3NvdXJjZT1ieXRlcw==). Surprisingly, it’s _not_ because they were spending all their time doodling cute hedgehogs – but because of some key technical challenges that make building replay for mobile a lot harder than building it for web. \[sponsored\]
    
8.  pretzelhammer wrote about [using Rust in non-Rust servers to improve performance](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/n2hohqu3zoox7rc6/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL3ByZXR6ZWxoYW1tZXIvcnVzdC1ibG9nL2Jsb2IvbWFzdGVyL3Bvc3RzL3J1c3QtaW4tbm9uLXJ1c3Qtc2VydmVycy5tZA==). “The Rust will continue until morale performance improves.”
    
9.  Eric Bailey wrote about [making content-aware components](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/48hvh7ur422pxksx/aHR0cHM6Ly9waWNjYWxpbC5saS9ibG9nL21ha2luZy1jb250ZW50LWF3YXJlLWNvbXBvbmVudHMtdXNpbmctY3NzLWhhcy1ncmlkLWFuZC1xdWFudGl0eS1xdWVyaWVzLw==) using `:has()`, grid, and quantity queries.
    
10.  The Svelte team released [a new CLI called `sv`](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/wnh2h6uweook0mi7/aHR0cHM6Ly9zdmVsdGUuZGV2L2Jsb2cvc3YtdGhlLXN2ZWx0ZS1jbGk=), which merges their previous CLIs and comes with built-in add-ons for formatting, linting, testing, auth and more. It’s so cool that it actually made me want to go as Rich Harris for Halloween, so I’ll need to find an accent coach, a hair stylist, and someone to sell my previous costume to asap.

7. Ian 和 Manoel 写了\[为什么 PostHog 花了这么长时间来构建移动会话重播]\(https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h3emmn3aqhp34x033hr/3ohphdu7edd2p4hr/aHR0cHM6Ly9wb3N0aG9nLmNvbS9ibG9nL21vYmlsZS1z ZXNzaW9uLXJlcGxheT91dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249Ynl0ZXMmdXRtX3NvdXJjZT1ieXRlcw==)。令人惊讶的是，这并不是因为他们把所有的时间都花在了可爱的刺猬上，而是因为一些关键的技术挑战使得为移动设备构建重播比为网络构建重播要困难得多。 \[赞助]
