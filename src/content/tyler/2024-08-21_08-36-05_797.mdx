## Cool Bits

1.  If you can’t wait to get your hands on the still-experimental React Compiler, I’d recommend poking around the [React Compiler playground](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/e0hph0u0mr7dm5h8/aHR0cHM6Ly9wbGF5Z3JvdW5kLnJlYWN0LmRldi8=) instead of adding it to prod. But I support your life choices either way.
    
2.  Split created this case study on [Moving Faster and Safer](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/7qh7h2uodn9xdduz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuc3BsaXQuaW8vZ2VvZmYtcmF5YmFjay1zb2Z0d2FyZS1kZXZlbG9wbWVudC1tYW5hZ2VyLWNhc2Utc3R1ZHkvP3V0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj1ieXRlc25ld3NsZXR0ZXJncmNzJnV0bV9tZWRpdW09Y29udGVudCUyMHN5bmRpY2F0aW9uJnV0bV9zb3VyY2U9Ynl0ZXM=) – which shows how they helped one team go from 2 deployments per day to _64 deployments per day_ by “learning to break things responsibly.” Sadly, they weren’t around back in 2011 to teach my college roommate how to “break things responsibly” when he was coming home drunk at 1:00 am 😭. \[sponsored\]
    
3.  Vercel just announced that [they raised $250 million](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/owhkhwurxqw0xpiv/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucmV1dGVycy5jb20vdGVjaG5vbG9neS92ZXJjZWwtY29tcGxldGVzLTI1MC1tbG4tc2VyaWVzLWUtcm91bmQtMzI1LWJsbi12YWx1YXRpb24tMjAyNC0wNS0xNi8=). And if that doesn’t convince your manager to let you start using server components, nothing will.
    
4.  Suni Pail gave a great talk about [real-time React Server Components](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/z2hgh7u35lem56fp/aHR0cHM6Ly95b3V0dS5iZS9UOFRaUTZrNFNMRT90PTI0MDcx) with PartyKit.
    
5.  Datadog created this free report on [10 insights of real-world container usage](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/p8hehquzrq4profq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZGF0YWRvZ2hxLmNvbS9ieXRlcy8=) by examining over 2.4 _billion_ containers run by thousands of their customers. \[sponsored\]
    
6.  React Native’s new architecture is [now in beta](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/x0hph3uwkqe4k6f5/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL3JlYWN0d2cvcmVhY3QtbmF0aXZlLW5ldy1hcmNoaXRlY3R1cmUvZGlzY3Vzc2lvbnMvMTg5) and so close you can almost taste it, release your inhibitions, feel the rain on your skin.
    
7.  Lauren Tan shared some encouraging [React Compiler case studies](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/6qhehou7x9l3x8so/aHR0cHM6Ly95b3V0dS5iZS9UOFRaUTZrNFNMRT90PTEyMTA0) from its rollout to various apps at Meta.
    
8.  [The React Team Q&A](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/kkhmh2ulzxnozkal/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1UOFRaUTZrNFNMRQ==) gave some interesting insight about the future of React and featured an unintentional Jets-vs-Sharks walkout moment between the Vercel and Meta team members.
    
9.  Get [high-quality QA services for your dev team](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/58hvh8um36g436u6/aHR0cHM6Ly9jYXJib25xYS5jb20vP3V0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj1jb29sX2JpdHMmdXRtX2NvbnRlbnQ9bWF5MTYmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1lbWFpbCZ1dG1fc291cmNlPWJ5dGVz) with CarbonQA. Their US-based testers will break your app repeatedly, so you never have to waste eng time on QA testing again. \[sponsored\]
    
10.  They open-sourced the [React Conf App](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hk7e29kuzhqk4v666bn/25h2h9u2lm3qlqb3/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL2V4cG8vcmVhY3QtY29uZi1hcHA=), so you can feel like you’re _really_ there. And for the full Las Vegas React Conf experience, you could spend $999 on a fake “ticket”, stay up til 4 am playing online poker, and start chain smoking with all the windows closed in your house.
