The Cloudflare team wrote about [How to build and deploy remote MCP servers to Cloudflare](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uo5qodl4s0hokeeo5bluz87o444sl/25h2h9u3v329dpf3/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNsb3VkZmxhcmUuY29tL3JlbW90ZS1tb2RlbC1jb250ZXh0LXByb3RvY29sLXNlcnZlcnMtbWNwLw==). [Gemini 2.5](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uo5qodl4s0hokeeo5bluz87o444sl/qvh8h8ud7drwn9ul/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmdvb2dsZS90ZWNobm9sb2d5L2dvb2dsZS1kZWVwbWluZC9nZW1pbmktbW9kZWwtdGhpbmtpbmctdXBkYXRlcy1tYXJjaC0yMDI1LyNnZW1pbmktMi01LXRoaW5raW5n) just came out and claims to be Google’s “most intelligent AI model,” because that’s usually how new releases work.

Ankit Maloo wrote an article called [The Bitter Lesson: rethinking how we build AI systems](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uo5qodl4s0hokeeo5bluz87o444sl/3ohphdu3o37mz8ar/aHR0cHM6Ly9hbmtpdG1hbG9vLmNvbS9iaXR0ZXItbGVzc29uLw==). The bitterest lesson of all is realizing that none of us will be spared from the singularity, no matter how many times we tell Claude “thank you.”
    
9.  TkDodo gave a conference talk on [lessons he’s learned from React Query’s API design](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/d0uo5qodl4s0hokeeo5bluz87o444sl/48hvh7um5mrlkzbx/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_YWJfY2hhbm5lbD1CZUpTJmluZGV4PTIwJmxpc3Q9UEw1M1oweXlZbnBXaXRQOFp2MDFUU0VRbUtMdnVSaF9EaiZ2PWwzUHhFcmNLZUFJ) while maintaining the library for the past few years.
