Marvin Hagemeister wrote about [Semver](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/zlun03nqdktnhk36qqmaphw3gxg00t6h3wl0/9qhzhdudgnwrzzf9/aHR0cHM6Ly9tYXJ2aW5oLmRldi9ibG9nL3NwZWVkaW5nLXVwLWphdmFzY3JpcHQtZWNvc3lzdGVtLXBhcnQtMTIv) in this latest entry to his series on Speeding up the JavaScript Ecosystem. Initially, I heard he was planning to call these blog posts _A Series of Unfortunate Events_ – but Lemony Snicket threatened to go full Oracle on him if he didn’t change the name.
    
9.  Rahul M. Juliato wrote this step-by-step guide to [unlocking Web Workers with React](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/zlun03nqdktnhk36qqmaphw3gxg00t6h3wl0/3ohphdu3qvmg4zbr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucmFodWxqdWxpYXRvLmNvbS9wb3N0cy9yZWFjdC13b3JrZXJz).
    
10.  Someone created a [Minecraft MCP Server](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/zlun03nqdktnhk36qqmaphw3gxg00t6h3wl0/n2hohquvneq06ei6/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL3l1bmlrby1zb2Z0d2FyZS9taW5lY3JhZnQtbWNwLXNlcnZlcg==) that allows AI assistants to control a Minecraft character in real time, so it can build structures, explore the world, etc. I can’t _wait_ until we can train AI to play all my video games for me so I have more time to work in the real mines.
