## Cool Bits

1.  Sentry just launched [Fair Source software licensing](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/6qhehou7e03p39fo/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLnNlbnRyeS5pby9zZW50cnktaXMtbm93LWZhaXItc291cmNlLw==) that aims to provide a sort of middle ground licensing option for software companies with OSS aspirations.
    
2.  Have you ever wondered what a gradient generator would look like if it was built by someone who was 1. really smart and 2. had a lot of (I assume) free time? Well, [now you know](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/kkhmh2ulv4o8oohl/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubGVhcm51aS5kZXNpZ24vdG9vbHMvZ3JhZGllbnQtZ2VuZXJhdG9yLmh0bWw=).
    
3.  [QA Wolf](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/58hvh8um2l454wu6/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucWF3b2xmLmNvbS8_dXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPVNsb3dRQUN5Y2xlczA4MTIyMDI0JnV0bV9tZWRpdW09bmV3c2xldHRlciZ1dG1fc291cmNlPWJ5dGVz) writes hundreds of tests for your product in Playwright, maintains all of those tests for you as your product scales, and provides the infrastructure to let you run 100% of your test suite in parallel – so your team can ship faster, with confidence. It feels like magic. \[sponsored\]
    
4.  Chevy Ray wrote about [how they created 175 fonts using Rust](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/25h2h9u2wnq7q5s3/aHR0cHM6Ly9jaGV2eXJheS5kZXYvYmxvZy9jcmVhdGluZy0xNzUtZm9udHMv). 6.  ViteConf just announced their [speaker list](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/g3hnhwu3wx0e2dar/aHR0cHM6Ly92aXRlY29uZi5vcmcv) which includes creators and maintainers from just about every interesting project in the JavaScript ecosystem. Rumor has it even Snoop Dogg himself will be attending (that’s a lie I made it up).
    
7.  [usBabyNames.js](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/9qhzhduprneg86i9/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL2pvbnJvaWcvdXNCYWJ5TmFtZXMuanM=) is a Node.js/promise-based data provider about baby name usage in the US from 1880-2023. S/O to all the “Herbert”s who are still around (and RIP to those who aren’t 🍻).
    
8.  Raygun (the monitoring platform, not the breakdancer) just released a fascinating study that [benchmarks the various OpenAI LLMs for automated error resolution](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/3ohphdu7gvkq8rur/aHR0cHM6Ly9yYXlndW4uY29tL2Jsb2cvYmVuY2htYXJraW5nLW9wZW5haS1tb2RlbHMtZm9yLWF1dG9tYXRlZC1lcnJvci1yZXNvbHV0aW9uLz91dG1fY2FtcGFpZ249YnJhbmQmdXRtX2NvbnRlbnQ9YmxvZyZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPXNwb25zb3JzaGlwJnV0bV9zb3VyY2U9Ynl0ZXM=). 9.  Ethan Niser wrote about [Effect Best Practices](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/n2hohqu30eln45u6/aHR0cHM6Ly9ldGhhbm5pc2VyLmRldi9ibG9nL2VmZmVjdC1iZXN0LXByYWN0aWNlcy8=). Not to be outdone…
    
10.  We also wrote about [best practices for managing effects](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/0vuvlov37di9hol7lvmapuv57o555fn/48hvh7ur8k703nix/aHR0cHM6Ly91aS5kZXYvYy9yZWFjdC9lZmZlY3RzP3M9Ynl0ZXM=) (but in React).
