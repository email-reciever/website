1.  Samuel wrote about [some changes their team is making to BlueSky’s React Native app](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/z2hgh7u3q375nmsp/aHR0cHM6Ly9tb3p6aXVzLmRldi9wb3N0LzNsNzc3bmh6NGgzMnc=) to make it feel more native. 4.  Brandon Roberts [added support for Web Sockets to Analog](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/6qhehou7m7wxrvfo/aHR0cHM6Ly9hbmFsb2dqcy5vcmcvZG9jcy9mZWF0dXJlcy9hcGkvd2Vic29ja2V0cw==), the full-stack Angular framework. 5.  Geoff Graham wrote about [unleashing the power of scroll driven animations](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/e0hph0u080emvpu8/aHR0cHM6Ly9jc3MtdHJpY2tzLmNvbS91bmxlYXNoLXRoZS1wb3dlci1vZi1zY3JvbGwtZHJpdmVuLWFuaW1hdGlvbnMv) on the recently resurrected CSS Tricks blog. 6.  The Cloudflare team published a blog post about [“fragment piercing” and “micro frontends”](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/7qh7h2uoro8dl6iz/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLmNsb3VkZmxhcmUuY29tL2ZyYWdtZW50LXBpZXJjaW5nLw==). 7.  [NativeWind just released v4.1](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/owhkhwur2rnx9nfv/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmF0aXZld2luZC5kZXYvYmxvZy9hbm5vdW5jZW1lbnQtbmF0aXZld2luZC12NC0x) of its library that lets you use Tailwind CSS in React Native.
    
8.  Maxime Heckel wrote an in-depth article about [crafting painterly shaders](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/z2hgh7u3q3756rtp/aHR0cHM6Ly9ibG9nLm1heGltZWhlY2tlbC5jb20vcG9zdHMvb24tY3JhZnRpbmctcGFpbnRlcmx5LXNoYWRlcnMv). 10.  Dev Agrawal created [Solid Socket](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/75u9w592ens8hke50l9s9uw6k7r66cn/x0hph3uw5wzk27h5/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL2RldmFncmF3YWwwOS9zb2xpZC1zb2NrZXQ=), an experimental new library that brings signals to WebSockets. I’m still waiting for him to publish `Solid-Pocket` and finally bring signals into the _Polly Pocket_ cinematic universe.
