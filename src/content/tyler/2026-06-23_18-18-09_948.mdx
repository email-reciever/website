## Cool Bits

1.  Astro just released [version 7.0.0](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/kkhmh2un03dz73hlh7/aHR0cHM6Ly9hc3Ryby5idWlsZC9ibG9nL2FzdHJvLTcv) which features an upgrade to Vite 8, a shiny new Rust compiler, and full access to the request pipeline. It’s basically everything I wanted for my 7th birthday.
    
2.  Speaking of Vite 8, the team just released version 8.1 which has [“full bundle mode”](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/58hvh8ugdzv3e9t6h4/aHR0cHM6Ly92aXRlLmRldi9ibG9nL2Fubm91bmNpbmctdml0ZTgtMQ==) (now called “experimental bundled dev mode”). If watching Tropic Thunder taught me anything, you never go full bundle mode.
    
3.  Learn how Datadog’s [Digital Experience Monitoring suite](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/25h2h9u3pevl57u3h4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZGF0YWRvZ2hxLmNvbS9yZXNvdXJjZXMvZGlnaXRhbC1leHBlcmllbmNlLW1vbml0b3Jpbmctc29sdXRpb24tYnJpZWYvP3V0bV9zb3VyY2U9Ynl0ZXNkZXYmdXRtX21lZGl1bT1uZXdzbGV0dGVyJnV0bV9jYW1wYWlnbj1kZy1jb250ZW50LXNvbHV0aW9uYnJpZWYtMjAyNmRpZ2l0YWxleHBlcmllbmNlbW9uaXRvcmluZy1kZW0tbXVsdGktd3ctZW4tNzAxdnkwMDAwMHEwcnk1eWFpLQ==) can help teams troubleshoot frontend issues, collaborate more efficiently, and deliver a strong user experience. \[sponsored\]
    
4.  Our resident Defense Against the Dark Arts professor, Una Kravets, wrote about how to do [modern CSS theming with `light-dark()`, `contrast-color()`, and `style-queries`](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/qvh8h8udm07exrulhk/aHR0cHM6Ly91bmEuaW0vbW9kZXJuLWNzcy10aGVtaW5n).
    
5.  Armin Ronacher wrote about his [mixed results using `/loop`](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/9qhzhdudvlq47zf9h3/aHR0cHM6Ly9sdWN1bXIucG9jb28ub3JnLzIwMjYvNi8yMy90aGUtY29taW5nLWxvb3Av) and how it’s probably the future whether we like it or not. As someone who watched a rogue `useEffect` take down Cloudflare’s API, all I can say is “good luck with that”.
    
6.  The `ast-grep` team added [support for outlining](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/3ohphdu35noxwzcrhn/aHR0cHM6Ly9hc3QtZ3JlcC5naXRodWIuaW8vYmxvZy9hc3QtZ3JlcC1vdXRsaW5lLmh0bWw=), which lets agents inspect the contents of a file without having to read the whole thing. This will probably save you a bunch of tokens, but also… Jevons paradox.
    
7.  Trigger.dev unifies your queues, crons, and scheduled tasks into a single managed TypeScript platform, complete with built-in observability and real-time streaming straight to your UI. [Deploy your first task today](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/n2hohquv9k82deb6hg/aHR0cHM6Ly9mYW5kZi5jby80eGFJY3BE). \[sponsored\]
    
8.  Victor Savkin wrote a post [reflecting on why React won the front-end framework wars](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/48hvh7umdn5wowuxh7/aHR0cHM6Ly9ueC5kZXYvYmxvZy9tZXRhLWhhcm5lc3Nlcy1hZ2VudHMtYW5kLWxlc3NvbnMtZnJvbS10aGUtZnJhbWV3b3JrLXdhcnM=) and what they can teach us about building agent harnesses.
    
9.  Aiden Bai just shipped [`cnfast`](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/wnh2h6uq235mzna7hx/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL2FpZGVueWJhaS9jbmZhc3Q=), an alternative to `tailwind-merge`, `clsx`, and `classnames` that’s up to 7x faster.
    
10.  David East shipped a “side project” called `inbrowser-agent` that [lets you run a full AI stack (including inference) in the browser](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/reh8h9umle2k8df2h6/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tL2RhdmlkZWFzdC9pbmJyb3dzZXItYWdlbnQ=). It seems like he might be trying to fill a Nikola Jokic sized hole in his heart.
    
11.  Kevin Van Cott wrote about how they [reduced TanStack Table’s memory footprint by 90%](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/08hwhgu26kon8rclh5/aHR0cHM6Ly90YW5zdGFjay5jb20vYmxvZy90YW5zdGFjay10YWJsZS12OS1tZW1vcnktcGVyZm9ybWFuY2U=) for large tables in version 9.
    
12.  After years of monorepomy, the team at Nx is [embracing the polyrepo lifestyle](https://c5e21242.click.convertkit-mail.com/p9unqxnv5mt9h2n3v9mhqhpqgg9pnirhg6ep/l2heh6ulmz0qp8u6h0/aHR0cHM6Ly90cnlwb2x5Z3JhcGguY29tLw==). All repos are now invited to join the polycule Polygraph.
