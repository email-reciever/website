## Cool Bits

1.  Baldur Bjarnason wrote a long article on [the parallel evolution of React and Web Components](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvx0gn4u8ug4om344hg/g3hnhwu3854ol4tr/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYmFsZHVyYmphcm5hc29uLmNvbS8yMDI0L2xpc2tvdnMtZ3VuLw==), in light of the most recent culture war. 4.  bobae kang wrote about [Serverless servers and the challenge of new React architecture](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/27ur84roxviohvx0gn4u8ug4om344hg/n2hohqu39pz5x4a6/aHR0cHM6Ly9ib2JhZWthbmcuY29tL2Jsb2cvc2VydmVybGVzcy1zZXJ2ZXJzLWFuZC10aGUtY2hhbGxlbmdlLW9mLW5ldy1yZWFjdC1hcmNoaXRlY3R1cmUv).
