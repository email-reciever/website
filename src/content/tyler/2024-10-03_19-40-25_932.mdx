## Cool Bits

1.  [ViteConf is live](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/l2heh6u3mow0kwf6/aHR0cHM6Ly92aXRlY29uZi5vcmcvbGl2ZQ==) and happening right now if you want to tune in. I tried using FanDuel to live-bet on how many times the speakers would say “blazing”, but I think that integration is broken.
    
2.  Amelia Wattenberger wrote a beautifully visual article on [bridging the gap between the hard logic of machines and the soft logic of humans](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/m2h7h6u86owgn2cm/aHR0cHM6Ly93YXR0ZW5iZXJnZXIuY29tL3Rob3VnaHRzL2hhcmQtYW5kLXNvZnQ=) with your UI.

The last time my therapist told me to “visualize my issues,” I didn’t go outside for three days, so hopefully this experience will be more helpful.
    
4.  Here’s an early New Year’s resolution – stop wasting your time on manual tests and [get QA Wolf to give you a personalized demo](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/7qh7h2uo203k45uz/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucWF3b2xmLmNvbS8_dXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPUN1dFlvdXJRQUN5Y2xlczEwMDMyMDI0JnV0bV9tZWRpdW09bmV3c2xldHRlciZ1dG1fc291cmNlPWJ5dGVz) of how they can help your team achieve 80% automated test coverage and ship 5x faster. 5.  ESLint now officially supports [linting JSON and Markdown](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/owhkhwurm45k8vbv/aHR0cHM6Ly9lc2xpbnQub3JnL2Jsb2cvMjAyNC8xMC9lc2xpbnQtanNvbi1tYXJrZG93bi1zdXBwb3J0Lw==).
    
6.  Devon Govett gave a great talk on [the past, present, and future of JavaScript bundlers](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/z2hgh7u32o04d2hp/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_dj1KVVM2RVBNYmswVQ==).
    
7.  [Eleventy v3.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/x0hph3uwvnxr7qs5/aHR0cHM6Ly9naXRodWIuY29tLzExdHkvZWxldmVudHkvcmVsZWFzZXMvdGFnL3YzLjAuMA==) was released full ESM support, async configuration, new Deno support, and more.
    
8.  Datadog’s team created this [Front-end Developer Kit](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/6qhehou78p0k23io/aHR0cHM6Ly9kYXRhZG9naHEuY29tL2J5dGVz) with a bunch of free resources to help you better understand user activity and troubleshoot front-end issues _before_ you crash prod, ideally. \[sponsored\]
    
9.  [Tauri 2.0](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/kkhmh2ul084d9ptl/aHR0cHM6Ly92Mi50YXVyaS5hcHAvYmxvZy90YXVyaS0yMC8=) comes with hot module replacement, a more advanced plugin system, and some noticeable DX improvements.
    
10.  Vite just [launched a company called VoidZero](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/4zum43m267beh57x6dmsqu6708m77t5/58hvh8umd5lv9es6/aHR0cHM6Ly92b2lkemVyby5kZXYvcG9zdHMvYW5ub3VuY2luZy12b2lkemVyby1pbmM=) and raised $4.6m from investors. You might not _love_ the idea of VC-funded open source, but it’s probably more sustainable than Evan You releasing a wallpaper app with a bunch of high-res photos of the Vite maintainers that costs $13.99/month.
