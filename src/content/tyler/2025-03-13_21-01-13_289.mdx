1.  If you want to go deeper on TypeScript’s jump to native code, the Syntax Pod just interviewed [Anders Hejlsberg and Daniel Rosenwasser](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/p8hehqu4kqqzmniq/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cueW91dHViZS5jb20vd2F0Y2g_YWJfY2hhbm5lbD1TeW50YXgmdj1abEd6YTRvSWxlWQ==) – aka the John Stockton and Karl Malone of TypeScript. 3.  [Developers vs. Managers](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/6qhehoulv997z3ho/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ2l0a3Jha2VuLmNvbS93ZWJpbmFyL2RldmVsb3BlcnMtdnMtbGVhZGVyc2hpcC11bmxvY2tpbmctYmV0dGVyLXByb2R1Y3Rpdml0eS13aXRoLWJldHRlci1kZXZleD9wcm9kdWN0PWdpdGtyYWtlbiZzb3VyY2U9ZW1haWwmdXRtX2NhbXBhaWduPTk3NTQ0NjgtTW9kdXMlMjBBdGxhc3NpYW4lMjBXZWJpbmFyJnV0bV9jb250ZW50PXNwb25zb3JlZCZ1dG1fbWVkaXVtPWVtYWlsJnV0bV9zb3VyY2U9Ynl0ZXNkZXYmdXRtX3Rlcm09) is a live fireside chat that GitKraken is hosting to see who really has the best ideas for improving developer experience and productivity. Embeddings (and how to create them) are perhaps the most interesting idea behind LLMs. In [this video](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/kkhmh2ungxxlrpil/aHR0cHM6Ly94LmNvbS9zdnBpbm8vc3RhdHVzLzE4OTMyOTIzODY4NTczOTg2NTI=), Santiago will help you understand them from scratch.
    
5.  Aiden Bai just completed another side quest by launching [same.dev](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/58hvh8ugp66mqei6/aHR0cHM6Ly9zYW1lLmRldi8=), which can “one-shot” clone any website.
    
6.  Augment created [the first AI coding assistant for large codebases](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/25h2h9u3rmm29rh3/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuYXVnbWVudGNvZGUuY29tLz9yZWY9Ynl0ZXM=). It helps you master your team’s most complex repos, it understands your team’s best practices, and it’s fully compatible with your existing IDE and other tools (VSCode, JetBrains, Vim, GitHub, etc). \[sponsored\]
    
7.  Daishi Kato wrote about how his state management library, Jotai [conceptually functions like JavaScript’s WeakMap API](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/qvh8h8udgkkrw7al/aHR0cHM6Ly9uZXdzbGV0dGVyLmRhaXNoaWthdG8uY29tL3AvaG93LXRoZS1qb3RhaS1zdG9yZS1hcGktaXMtaW5zcGlyZWQtYnktdGhlLXdlYWttYXAtYXBp). 9.  The Web Almanac just dropped their chapter on [JavaScript](https://click.convertkit-mail4.com/mvu5nv5m3wa5hqqrr3gulur86oqqqt3/3ohphdu36pp7m9tr/aHR0cHM6Ly9hbG1hbmFjLmh0dHBhcmNoaXZlLm9yZy9lbi8yMDI0L2phdmFzY3JpcHQ=). It’s perfect for my family members who’ve read Bytes for years and still have no idea what I’m talking about.
