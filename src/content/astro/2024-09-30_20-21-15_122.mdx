It's time for some exciting updates about Astro, Starlight, and a recap of everything you might have missed from the last month including our biggest release yet: Astro v5.0 beta!

## Notable Releases

*   Astro [5.0 beta](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/astro.build%2Fblog%2Fastro-5-beta%2F%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-september-2024/bP1H/Zl_4AQ/AQ/2dc720ee-5de7-46a0-b7c6-e1930dc53b01/3/fi2iSJJ2hi) was released in September! See the [v5 beta upgrade guide](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/5-0-0-beta.docs.astro.build%2Fen%2Fguides%2Fupgrade-to%2Fv5%2F%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-september-2024/bP1H/Zl_4AQ/AQ/2dc720ee-5de7-46a0-b7c6-e1930dc53b01/4/TCjaP_5gbu) to try this early preview that brings stable Content Layer API, server islands, type-safe environment variables, and more!
*   [Starlight 0.28](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/astro.build%2Fblog%2Fstarlight-028%2F%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-september-2024/bP1H/Zl_4AQ/AQ/2dc720ee-5de7-46a0-b7c6-e1930dc53b01/5/X7U6BRfXvW) brings next-level localization and on-demand rendering support, as well as comprehensive documentation for all of Starlight's built-in components.
