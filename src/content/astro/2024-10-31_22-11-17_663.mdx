## Notable Releases

*   Astro [5.0 beta](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/astro.build%2Fblog%2Fastro-5-beta%2F%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-october-2024/bP1H/NA65AQ/AQ/9a7a02d6-70c2-49e6-9c28-30d4464a2744/3/wKCgsDR40A) has seen several releases this month: we're up to `astro@5.0.0-beta.6`! See the [v5 beta upgrade guide](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/5-0-0-beta.docs.astro.build%2Fen%2Fguides%2Fupgrade-to%2Fv5%2F%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-october-2024/bP1H/NA65AQ/AQ/9a7a02d6-70c2-49e6-9c28-30d4464a2744/4/KGMPn3VnVL) to try this early preview that brings stable Content Layer API, server islands, type-safe environment variables, and more!
*   Of course, there's still more coming on the stable branch: [Astro 4.16](https://click.pstmrk.it/3s/github.com%2Fwithastro%2Fastro%2Freleases%2Ftag%2Fastro%25404.16.0%3Futm_source%3Dwithastro%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dthe-astro-post-october-2024/bP1H/NA65AQ/AQ/9a7a02d6-70c2-49e6-9c28-30d4464a2744/5/hv7L_Qqg3L) launched with an option for concurrent builds, Shiki language aliases, bug fixes, and more!
