Robin Wieruch

Kyle Gill

[![](https://res.cloudinary.com/cpress/image/upload/c_limit,w_480,h_480,q_auto/copm/4b0815fd.png)](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167012/5d0bbb4a61)

[KendoReact Free is Here, npm-Install Available!](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167012/5d0bbb4a61 "www.telerik.com") — 50+ robust KendoReact components, including React Data Grid, DatePicker and DropDownList. No sign-ups, trials, or licenses required - you can use KendoReact Free in production, to experiment, or to quickly see what it's all about!

Progress KendoReact sponsor

**IN BRIEF:**

*   [Oxlint, a Rust-powered JavaScript and TSX linter, is now in beta.](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167015/5d0bbb4a61) It has over 500 rules out of the box, including plenty for React-specific use cases, and manages to lint Microsoft's entire VS Code repo in under a second.
    
*   [Netlify is now TanStack's 'official deployment partner'](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167016/5d0bbb4a61).
    
*   [Parcel v2.14.0 has been released](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167017/5d0bbb4a61) with beta support for React Server Components.
    

[The Great ESLint v9 Migration Adventure: A Developer's Survival Guide](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167018/5d0bbb4a61 "www.neoxs.me") — _“Let’s just say… I’ve seen things. I hit all the bumps, took all the wrong turns, and somehow made it to the other side—so you don’t have to.”_

Yacine Kharoubi

[SSR Deep Dive for React Developers](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167019/5d0bbb4a61 "www.developerway.com") — Explore step-by-step how Server-Side Rendering (SSR), pre-rendering, hydration, and Static Site Generation (SSG) work in React, their costs, performance impact, benefits, and trade-offs.

Nadia Makarevich

📄 [Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Node.js, Podman AI Lab and React](https://react.statuscode.com/link/167022/5d0bbb4a61) Michael Dawson (Red Hat)
