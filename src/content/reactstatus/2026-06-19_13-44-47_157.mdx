**IN BRIEF:**

*   **The [Rust-port of React Compiler](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186823/5d0bbb4a61) has been merged!** Support is already landing in popular projects, like [in Next.js](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186824/5d0bbb4a61) (already in [canary](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186825/5d0bbb4a61)), [Oxlint 1.70](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186826/5d0bbb4a61), [swc](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186827/5d0bbb4a61) and [Rolldown](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186828/5d0bbb4a61).
    
*   [React Native 0.86](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186829/5d0bbb4a61) has been released with edge-to-edge support on Android 15+ and DevTools improvements.
    
*   WordPress announced it's [upgrading to React 19](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186830/5d0bbb4a61) in WordPress 7.1, but [this has now been temporarily reverted](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186831/5d0bbb4a61) due to plugin compatibility problems.
    
*   📊 Expo has unveiled [a (free) open beta of _EAS Observe_](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186832/5d0bbb4a61), its performance monitoring/observability service for React Native apps.
    
*   The [release candidate of TypeScript 7.0](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186833/5d0bbb4a61) has been released.
    

[Can Signals Make _React Redux_ Faster at Scale?](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186834/5d0bbb4a61 "github.com") — A PR by Mark Erikson where he experiments with signals-based `SignalProvider` and `useSignalSelector` drop-ins that only re-run selectors for components whose state actually changed, with some noteworthy performance gains.

Mark Erikson

[Wasp Now Lets You Write Your Full-Stack Logic as a TypeScript Spec](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186835/5d0bbb4a61 "wasp.sh") — The ‘batteries-included’ React, Node and Prisma [Wasp](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186836/5d0bbb4a61) full-stack framework has switched its spec layer from its own custom language to TypeScript. Specify the shape of your app [like this](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186837/5d0bbb4a61) and Wasp compiles it, with your frontend/backend code, into a full-stack app.

Martin Sosic (Wasp)

[That Stale Dashboard Isn't a React Problem](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186838/5d0bbb4a61 "www.tigerdata.com") — Real-time components need real-time data. Tiger Data keeps your Postgres queries fresh without pipelines or polling.

Tiger Data (creators of TimescaleDB) sponsor

📄 [How We Cut Slow Responses by 80% Migrating to Next.js App Router](https://react.statuscode.com/link/186840/5d0bbb4a61) – A classifieds site moved from Pages Router to App Router, cut slow responses, and tackled some Nginx and Akamai HTML streaming gotchas. Francesca Milan (Subito)
