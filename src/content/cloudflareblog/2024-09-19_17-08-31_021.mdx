[How Cloudflare is helping domain owners with the upcoming Entrust CA distrust by Chrome and Mozilla](https://content.cloudflare.com/NzEzLVhTQy05MTgAAAGVqjnYzO4pHd6AT5NPuHAL70Rpo9qC9ME5v_nLB_ocdDeQegrHSsy2J2pq4mY90UTow3tpeiQ=)

Chrome and Mozilla will stop trusting Entrust’s public TLS certificates issued after November 2024 due to concerns about Entrust’s compliance with security standards. In response, Entrust is partnering with SSL.com to continue providing trusted certificates. Cloudflare will support SSL.com as a CA, simplifying certificate management for customers using Entrust by automating issuance and renewals.

By Dina Kozlov

[Cloudflare 如何帮助域名所有者应对 Chrome 和 Mozilla 即将推出的 Entrust CA 不信任](https://content.cloudflare.com/NzEzLVhTQy05MTgAAAGVqjnYzO4pHd6AT5NPuHAL70Rpo9qC9ME5v_nLB_ocdDeQegrHSsy2J2pq4mY90UTow3tpeiQ=)

由于担心 Entrust 遵守安全标准，Chrome 和 Mozilla 将停止信任 Entrust 在 2024 年 11 月之后颁发的公共 TLS 证书。作为回应，Entrust 正在与 SSL.com 合作，继续提供可信证书。 Cloudflare 将支持 SSL.com 作为 CA，通过自动颁发和续订来简化使用 Entrust 的客户的证书管理。

迪娜·科兹洛夫
