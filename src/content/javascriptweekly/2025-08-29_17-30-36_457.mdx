[Tests Are Dead. Meticulous AI Is Here](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173538/d076523b1d "www.meticulous.ai") — Meticulous creates and maintains a continuously evolving E2E UI test suite with zero developer effort. Meticulous AI sponsor

[New in ESLint v9.34.0: Multithread Linting](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173539/d076523b1d "eslint.org") — A common feature of tools like [Rslint](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173540/d076523b1d), [Biome](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173541/d076523b1d), and [Oxlint](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173542/d076523b1d), is being '_N_x times faster' than ESLint, but the [latest ESLint](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/173543/d076523b1d) has a trick up its sleeve and can now process multiple files at the same time, dramatically reducing lint times for large projects.

Francesco Trotta
