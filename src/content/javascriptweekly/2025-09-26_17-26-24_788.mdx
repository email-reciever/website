* Digging into [plain objects](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174900/d076523b1d), [`Array`s](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174901/d076523b1d), [`Map`s](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174902/d076523b1d), and [exceptions.](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174903/d076523b1d)
* An [introduction to frontend frameworks](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174904/d076523b1d) using Preact.
* [Installing npm packages and bundling.](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174905/d076523b1d)
* [Promises and `async` functions.](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/174906/d076523b1d)
