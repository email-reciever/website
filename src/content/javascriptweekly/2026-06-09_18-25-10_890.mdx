[VoidZero is Joining Cloudflare](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186265/d076523b1d "voidzero.dev") — One of the year’s big moves in JS tooling, as told by Evan himself. The company he founded to build Vite, Vitest, Rolldown, Oxc, etc. is joining Cloudflare. He’s candid about why (monetization is hard). Good news: all projects stay MIT-licensed, and Evan and co remain in charge.

Join live and chat directly with PostgreSQL speakers, other developers and users. There is also swag waiting for you. [Register for updates](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186264/d076523b1d).

Microsoft | AMD sponsor

[Announcing Angular v22](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186267/d076523b1d "blog.angular.dev") — Google’s batteries-included framework graduates three big features to stable/production-ready: [Signal Forms](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186268/d076523b1d), [Angular Aria](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186269/d076523b1d), and [asynchronous reactivity](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186270/d076523b1d). The router also adds experimental support for the native browser [Navigation API](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186271/d076523b1d).

The Angular Team

**IN BRIEF:**

*   Safari 27 (beta), announced at WWDC, [includes a standards-compliant ESM loader rewrite](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186272/d076523b1d) improving top-level `await` support, and [adds support for JSPI](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186273/d076523b1d) (JavaScript Promise Integration) in WebAssembly.
    
*   [Electron 43 is in beta](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186274/d076523b1d) with big perf boosts, thanks to an embedded Node.js snapshot, bundles cached as V8 bytecode, and link-time optimization on the main binary.
    
*   The [Rust port of _React Compiler_](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186275/d076523b1d) is ready to test (Meta is already seeing _"great results"_) – it's set to be merged imminently.
    

**RELEASES:**

*   [TanStack Table v9 Beta](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186276/d076523b1d) – A powerful headless engine for creating data grids with support for React, Preact, Solid, Vue, Angular, Svelte, and Lit.
    
*   [Node-RED 5.0](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186277/d076523b1d) – 'Low-code' flow programming environment, built on Node.js.
    
*   [Rolldown 1.1](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186278/d076523b1d), [pnpm 11.5](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186279/d076523b1d), [RunJS 4](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186280/d076523b1d), [Emscripten 6.0](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/186281/d076523b1d)
