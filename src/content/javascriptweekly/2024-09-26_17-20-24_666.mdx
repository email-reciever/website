[Deno 2.0 Release Candidate](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/160340/d076523b1d "deno.com") — Deno started life as a manifestation of ideas that Node’s original creator, Ryan Dahl, had after his experiences with Node. Deno 2 is the next step: how the Deno team thinks _Deno_ should ultimately be. Many changes await: `window` is gone and Node’s `process` appears, dependency management has been improved, numerous APIs have gone stable (e.g. WebGPU), and both Node.js API and CommonJS support have continued to improve.

Bartek Iwańczuk and Andy Jiang
