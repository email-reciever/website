It now has an easy way to [deploy Next.js apps to Cloudflare Workers](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/167996/d076523b1d), you can add a ['deploy to Workers' button](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/167997/d076523b1d) to your Git repos, [v1 of their Cloudflare Vite plugin](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/167998/d076523b1d) has been released, and you can now [deploy an entire frontend, backend, and database](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/167999/d076523b1d) (supporting React Router, Astro, Vue.js, Svelte and more) in one Worker.
    
*   [The RedwoodJS team has an update](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/168000/d076523b1d) on the future of Redwood and what the 'next epoch' holds for the React-based framework.
    
*   [p5.js](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/168001/d076523b1d) 2.0 looks like it'll be with us soon – its eighth beta has just landed. [The release notes](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/168002/d076523b1d) for beta 2 give an extensive idea of what's coming.
    
*   🇷🇴 The annual [JSHeroes conference](https://javascriptweekly.com/link/168003/d076523b1d) is taking place this May 29-30 in Cluj, Romania.
