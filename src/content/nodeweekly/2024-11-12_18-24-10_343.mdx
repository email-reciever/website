[Node v23.2.0 (Current) Released](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162110/0a4070b383 "nodejs.org") — On paper, a relatively minor release that updates the root certificates, adding five new ones, but [development of TypeScript support](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162111/0a4070b383) has now moved into _active_ development (as opposed to ‘early development’) and there’s [a new `module.stripTypeScriptTypes` API.](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162112/0a4070b383)

Antoine du Hamel

💡 Erick Wendel [▶️ recorded this helpful 18-minute video](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162113/0a4070b383) showing off Node's new TypeScript-specific functionality and even caught up with Node.js TSC member Marco Ippolito who's leading the type-stripping initiative.

[Introducing the `vlt` Package Manager](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162114/0a4070b383 "blog.vlt.sh") — Made up of an incredibly experienced team, including `npm`’s creator Isaac Schlueter, [Vlt](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162115/0a4070b383) set out to ‘build the future’ of JS package management. The `vlt` tool, explained here, is the first fruit of the project and acts as ‘a drop-in replacement’ to whatever you’re using now.

[Key Learnings From the State of DevSecOps Study](https://nodeweekly.com/link/162109/0a4070b383 "www.datadoghq.com") — To understand the state of DevSecOps adoption in 2024, Datadog evaluated real-world data from thousands of applications. Read the report for key insights on secure application development techniques to improve your security posture.
