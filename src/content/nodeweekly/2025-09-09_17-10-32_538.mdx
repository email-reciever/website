Sadly, a prolific package author (among [others](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173907/0a4070b383), like [DuckDB, who explain how the attack worked on them](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173963/0a4070b383)) [fell victim](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173908/0a4070b383) to the scam, resulting in some popular packages becoming compromised ([like Chalk](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173909/0a4070b383), [debug](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173910/0a4070b383), and [others](https://nodeweekly.com/link/173911/0a4070b383)).
